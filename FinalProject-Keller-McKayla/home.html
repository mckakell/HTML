French for Beginners

Salut! Welcome to my page. I have created this website as an educational tool for beginners learning French and as a review for intermediate speakers. As an Indiana University Informatics student with a French minor I have come across many educational French tools within the last year and most of them are online tools. These online tools can often times be hard to understand or even hard to look at with outdated design. That being said the goal of this website is to be a resource with good information and a good interface.
