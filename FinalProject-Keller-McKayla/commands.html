Making Commands in French

To order someone to do something, use the present tense of a verb in the tu, vous or nous form. If the verb form ends in es or as drop the final s.

Examples

-ER Verbs

Tu parles → Parle! (speak)

Vous parlez → Parlez! (speak)

Nous parlons → Parlons! (let's speak)

-IR Verbs

Tu finis → Finis! (finish)

Vous finissez → Finissez! (finish)

Nous finissons → Finissons! (let's finish)

-RE Verbs

Tu prends → Prends! (take)

Vous prenez → Prenez! (take)

Nous prenons → Prenons! (let's take)
